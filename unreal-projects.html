Unreal Engine Projects

Unreal Engine 5 Projects

Advanced Movement System

A comprehensive character movement pack leveraging Unreal Engine Blueprint system and animation morphing technology for seamless character movement.

Key Features

  • Dynamic running mechanics with speed adjustments
  • Smooth jumping system with air control
  • Fluid sliding mechanics
  • Advanced mantling system for obstacle traversal
  • Realistic climbing mechanics

Technical Implementation

The system utilizes animation morphing for seamless state transitions, creating a polished and professional feel. Implemented entirely using Unreal Engine's Blueprint visual scripting system, demonstrating expertise in both gameplay programming and animation implementation.

UEFN Projects

A collection of custom-designed maps that showcase my technical expertise in Unreal Engine for Fortnite (UEFN). Each project demonstrates my ability to integrate advanced mechanics, optimize gameplay, and create immersive experiences.

The Bridges (1v1)

A competitive, one-on-one battle set across dynamically shifting bridges. Players must outwit and outlast their opponent by mastering both combat and environmental navigation.

Key Features:
  • Custom Scoring System: Tracks player performance based on kills and objectives, providing a fair and balanced scoring model.
  • Dynamic Bridge Mechanics: Bridges change shape and functionality, requiring players to adapt to shifting battlegrounds.
  • Optimized for Competitive Play: Designed with competitive balance in mind, ensuring a challenging but fair experience for both players.

The Bridges (4v4v4v4)

A team-based, multi-team map that pits four squads of players against each other in a battle for dominance. The environment is built to promote strategic team coordination and high-stakes gameplay.

Key Features:
  • Multi-Team Management System: Supports up to four teams, each with distinct roles and objectives, fostering cooperative and competitive gameplay.
  • Advanced Spawn Logic: Smart spawning ensures players are placed in the best possible locations, minimizing spawn camping while promoting fair competition.
  • Team-Based Scoring Mechanics: Points are awarded based on team achievements, such as kills and objectives, fostering teamwork and strategy.

Horror Map

Step into a chilling world where tension and terror await at every corner. This map immerses players in a haunting atmosphere, combining environmental storytelling with trigger-based events to create a suspenseful horror experience.

Key Features:
  • Dynamic Lighting System: Lighting shifts in response to player actions, enhancing the eerie atmosphere and amplifying the sense of dread.
  • Trigger-Based Events: Key events are triggered by player movement and actions, ensuring a unique and unpredictable horror experience.
  • Atmospheric Audio Integration: Sounds play a critical role in creating a spine-chilling environment, with audio cues and ambient noise enhancing the suspense.

Fall Guys-Inspired Map

Inspired by the popular "Fall Guys" game, this map features a series of challenging, physics-based obstacles that players must navigate to reach the finish line. The competitive nature of the map keeps players engaged as they race against the clock.

Key Features:
  • Physics-Based Obstacles: Players must navigate moving platforms, swinging barriers, and other dynamic obstacles that rely on physics-based interactions.
  • Checkpoint System: Players can restart from checkpoints if they fall behind, ensuring that progress isn't lost while maintaining the challenge.
  • Time Trial Mechanics: Players race against the clock to complete the obstacle course, with the fastest times being rewarded with rankings and leaderboards.
